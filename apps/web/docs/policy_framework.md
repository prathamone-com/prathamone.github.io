## 1. The Principle of Sovereign Intelligence
PrathamOne operates on a **"Sovereign-First"** hybrid model. 
- **Data Locality:** Critical student performance data and local nuances reside on the student's device (SQLite/PWA Ledger).
- **Inference Routing:** Queries are prioritized for local sovereign models (DeepSeek/Llama) to minimize latency and maximize privacy.

---

## 2. Lecturer-in-the-Loop (LITL) Policy
To maintain 100% pedagogical trust, PrathamOne enforces the **Lecturer Override Policy**:
- **Verification Priority:** All AI-generated Core Theory and HOTS content must pass a second "Verifier Agent" check (Confidence Score > 85%).
- **Manual Override:** Lecturers have "Root Authority" to edit, flag, or overwrite any AI-generated response. 
- **Feedback Loop:** Lecturer edits are versioned and fed back into the "Grounded Knowledge Base" to refine future generations.

---

## 3. DPDP Act & Minor Data Compliance
In alignment with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act (2023):
- **Parental Consent:** Students under the age of 18 require a verified "Parent Account" for full platform access.
- **Data Minimization:** Modular architecture ensures that "Concept" and "Practice" views only receive anonymized context, never student PII.
- **Right to Erasure:** Parents and Students can request a full "Ledger Wipe" (Local + Cloud) at any time.

---

## 4. Multi-Agent Ecosystem Roles
PrathamOne is orchestrated by specialized functional agents, visualized in real-time to the student:

1.  **Lecture Composer**: Assembles text, formulas ($LaTeX$), and interactive examples into a cinematic flow.
2.  **Assessment Agent**: Generates adaptive difficulty quizzes (Easy, Medium, Hard, JEE/NEET).
3.  **Verifier Agent**: Cross-references AI output against the "Global Learning Ledger" and official board PDFs.
4.  **Pedagogical Optimizer**: Refines the instructional pace based on student interaction latency.
5.  **Recommendation Agent**: Analyzes "Weak Areas" and suggests remedial "Socratic Sprints."
6.  **Socratic Bridge Agent**: Triggers competitive **"THE PUSH"** mode when mastery exceeds 80%.

## 5. Modular View Orchestration Policy
To ensure "Sovereign UI" integrity:
- **Dashboard View**: Primary portal for activity history and cross-subject navigation.
- **Session View**: Active AI-orchestrated environment. Must display "Active Agent" metadata for functional transparency.
- **Static View**: Pre-verified pedagogical artifacts mapped to board standards. Used for "Offline Mode" instruction.

---

## 6. Monetization Ethics: "The Prepaid AI Economy"
- **Token Transparency**: AI compute is metered in "AI Tokens" visible in the student dashboard. 
- **Fair Access**: Free tiers are maintained for essential subjects to prevent a "Digital Knowledge Divide."
- **Institutional Autonomy**: Schools can white-label the platform and set their own credit/token limits.
